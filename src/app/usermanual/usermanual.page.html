Appointment Status

You can check appointment status on bocxy to view the number of new appointments,upcoming appointments,walkin customers, number of appointments

New Appointments :

New appointment report displays the new appointments that are booked from the customer end.

Upcoming :

Upcoming report displays once the new appointments accepted by the merchant.

Walkin Customers :

Walkin customers report displays the no of customers walked-in in a day, that are created by the merchant himself.

Cancelled :

Cancelled report displays the number of cancelled appointments

Staff Management :

Staff management offers you to add a new staff to your store and manage the existing staffs in your store.

Appointment Configuration :

Appointment configuration offers you to configure the time of appointments according to the slots and stylists availability in your store.

Store Time Management :

Store time management offers you to manage the timings of the store available.

Store Holiday Management :

Store holiday management offers you to manage the holiday schedules of the store.
